Who Are We

We are a team of cloud engineers at Lunixa Cloud Solutions (LCS), united by a deep passion for technology and a shared vision to lead digital transformation in the Kingdom. We leverage our expertise and innovation to empower organizations and companies, enabling them to achieve operational excellence through advanced and tailored cloud solutions.

LCS was founded on the belief that cloud transformation is not just a technical migration, but a strategic journey toward a more agile and innovative future. We are committed to delivering solutions that exceed expectations, support our clients' goals, and align with the Kingdom's aspirations.

Our mission is inspired by Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030, which motivates us to innovate, enhance efficiency, and develop national digital capabilities. Through our partnerships with clients, we contribute to building a diverse, knowledge-driven, and pioneering economy in the region.

At LCS, we don't just keep up with developments—we create them and lead the future of cloud in the Kingdom and beyond.

Why Work With Us

1. Full Saudi Team

As a fully Saudi-owned company, LCS is deeply aligned with Vision 2030, supporting national goals for digital transformation and local tech enablement. We understand the local market, culture, and regulatory needs better than global providers.
